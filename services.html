Workshop

PETROJET owns (5) workshops in Egypt, one in Iraq with total area of 175,840 m2 and one in Sultanate of Oman. All workshops are approved for ASME stamps (U, U2, PP-Stamp and S-Stamp) as well as the R-Stamp from the National Board of Boilers & Pressure Vessels and in addition to the API Monogram. (3) of them work in steel structure fabrication with total production capacity of 3,640 ton per month. Engineering activities are assisted with the latest versions of engineering software such as Autocad, Tekla, STAAD.Pro and Sap 2000. The workshops are continuously upgraded and developed to meet with the growing demands of projects requirements. We are the first manufacturer in Egypt and the second in Africa to be certified for ASME Nuclear (N, NPT, NS, NA) stamps.

Pipe Coating

PETROJET owns & operates (3) pipe coating plants in Suez and Port-Said to serve the projects’ needs of anti-corrosion pipeprotection in Egypt & abroad using different types of coating systems such as:
  1. External Anti-Corrosion Coating according to (ISO 21809-1/ DNV RP-106 / NF A49-710-711)
  2. Internal Flow Coating according to (API 5L2)
  3. Concrete Weight Coating according to (ISO 21809-5)
  4. Fusion Bond Epoxy according to (ISO 21809-2 / NACE RP 0394)

1- THE PORT SAID POLYETHYLENE COATING PLANT WAS ESTABLISHED IN 1985 AND COMPRISES (3) COATING LINES:

  1. P.E and P.P Anti-Corrosion coating Line Ranging: (3” -52”) Pipes Diameter with a coating capacity of 7000 m2/D
  2. Internal lining epoxy coating Line Ranging: (8” -52”) Pipes Diameter with a coating capacity of 6000 m2/D
  3. Fusion Bond Epoxy Coating Line Ranging: (3”-52”) Pipes Diameter with a coating capacity of 4000 m2/D

2- PORT SAID CONCRETE COATING PLANT WAS ESTABLISHED IN 1985 AND COMPRISES (2) COATING LINES:

  1. Polyurethane foam (PUF) Ranging (6”- 32”) Pipes Diameter with a coating capacity of 1100 m2/D
  2. Concrete Coating (2 Coating Lines) Ranging (6”- 48”) Pipes Diameter with a coating capacity of 2000 m2/D

3- THE SUEZ COATING PLANT WAS ESTABLISHED IN 1984 AND COMPRISES (3) COATING LINES:

  1. P.E and P.P Coating line Ranging (4”- 36”) Pipes Diameter with a coating capacity of 2000 m2/D
  2. Internal lining Ranging (12”-36”) Pipes Diameter with a coating capacity of 1500 m2/D
  3. Polyurethane foam (PUF) Ranging (6”- 32”) Pipes Diameter with a coating capacity of 1100m2/D

Badr Maintenance Workshops

It is a General Department of Equipment with an area of 80764 m2 which contains (4) maintenance workshops. These Workshops execute maintenance, repairs and renewing transportation equipment.

1 – Maintenance Workshops with an area of 7776 m2 including (7) Workshops:

  • Concrete Equipment repair
  • Metal Machining
  • Trailers and Semi-Trailers repair and welding
  • Truck and Diesel engines repair
  • Engines Overhauling
  • Electrical and Welding Equipment test and repair
