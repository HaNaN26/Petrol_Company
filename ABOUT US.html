Profile

WHO WE ARE?

PETROLEUM is a joint-stock multidisciplinary integrated contractor founded in Egypt in 1975 to be the construction arm of the Egyptian Petroleum sector. Today, after 4 decades, Petrojet is recognized as a leading regional EPC Contractor with a strong portfolio covering various businesses like Oil, Gas, Chemical, Petrochemical, Power, Industrial and Infrastructure projects. The company renders its services in numerous countries across the Middle East and North Africa, and is currently stretching its wings to Sub-Saharan Africa as well. The Company employs a workforce of over 36,000 people, and owns a large fleet of modern construction equipment, world-class fabrication facilities for Process Equipment, Offshore Fabrication Yards and pipe coating facilities.

WHAT WE DO?

Since its inception back in 1975, PETROJET has maintained a steady pace building a world class experience base across various strategic focus areas like Oil, Gas, Chemical, Petrochemical, Power, Industrial & Infrastructure Projects. PETROLEUM activities cover:EPC/Construction Civil, Mechanical and E&I of Oil Refineries, Oil Treatment, Gas Processing, Gas Liquefaction. (160 Plants) Cross-Country Pipelines. (30,000 KM) Storage Tank Farms. (300,000 TONS)
