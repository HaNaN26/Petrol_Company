PETROLEUM WINS MEED 2021 AWARD

Egyptian Oil & Gas Sector presented in petrojet wins MEED 2021 Projects Award for the best Oil & Gas project of the year for Ras Badran Tank Farm Petrojet succeeded in execution of the largest storage tank at Ras Badran with a height of 21.8 m, diameter of 110m and a total weight of 175m3 and a double deck floating roof using 240 hydraulic jacks synchronized together to ensure smooth raising. The tank is one of the 29 storage tanks executed in 4 different areas ( Ghareb- Agroud- Ras Badran- shokeir) it's worth noting that Petrojet won MEED Award in 2019 year for the Best international project outside Gulf Borders for execution Ismailia Tunnels

WE ARE OFFICIALLY ON GUINNESS BOOK OF RECORDS !!

We are officially on Guinness Book of Records !! Proud to announce that our “175,000 m3 (1.1 Million Barrels) Crude Oil Storage Tank” project in Ras Badran, Sinai, has officially entered The Guinness Book of Records. This tank is part of the national level project assigned by the Egyptian Ministry of Petroleum to Petrojet, as main EPC contractor. Petrojet succeeded in executing the storage tank with a height of about 21.83 meters, with a diameter of 110 meters, and a weight of more than 3300 tons. using a first of its kind technique to raise the stunning 1,000 Tons Double Deck Floating Roof from assembly level to maintenance level utilizing 240 hydraulic jacks synchronized together to ensure smooth raising of the deck.

Petrojet In EGYPS 2020

Under the patronage and support of His Excellency, President Abdel Fattah El Sisi inaugurated on Tuesday 11 feb the Egypt Petroleum Show (EGYPS 2020) unde the theme of "North Africa And the Mediterranean: Delivering The Needs of Tomorrow." More than 270 local and international industrial experts took part in the event along with 1,600 participants. Moreover, about 14 countries participated in the event alongside 15 international petroleum companies and more than 450 exhibitors. Prime Minister Moustafa Madbouli and Petroleum Minister Tareq el Molla attended the event as well as a number of ministers and statesmen. EGYPS 2020 discussed several topics related to the transformation of Egypt into a regional energy hub, the development of the exploration and production (E&P) activities, as well as refining and marketing activities. Moreover, the conference discussed as well the health, safety, and environment (HSE) activities, the role of women in the sector, and the youth empowerment to prepare them to be the next generation of leaders. El Molla stated that the conference is an important window to shed light on the sector’s accomplishments and unprecedented developments, in addition to showcasing the ministry’s plans and road maps for the future. Petrojet has participated in EGYPS 2020 exhibition with a unique booth that remarked a lot of important events inside its walls where Eng. Waleed Lofty Petrojet CEO had signed MOU with The Ministry of Mines and Hydrocarbons of the republic of Equatorial Guinea as Petrojet will be working on petroleum and refining projects in Equatorial Guinea for the first time, In a meeting with El-Molla, Brendan Bechtel, chairman and CEO of the Bechtel Group signed two cooperation agreements with Petrojet and Enppi regarding projects in refining and petrochemicals complex in the Red Sea. In addition Petrojet signed a contract for establishment of a new all-Egyptian company that will turn rice straw, into medium-density fiber board (MDF) wooden panels. The conference was followed by the highly anticipated 3rd annual Women in Energy Awards ceremony that has built upon the legacy of honoring and celebrating the achievements of exceptional women in the oil and gas sector, one of which was Petrojet’s media general manager who was awarded with “women in leadership” award In addition, Petrojet had a bronze sponsorship of EGYPS Youth Engagement Program that was held to provide an onsite learning platform, engaging with high school students within the framework of STEM Education focusing on Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics, to ultimately encourage them to pursue careers in the oil and gas industry. Petrojet had participated in this program through an interesting session showing students how to install an oil rig zone in addition to showing them all the activities of the company.
